The Three Best Movies According to Matt

My top 3 movies of all time

The Shawshank Redemption

A hard hitting story with a thrilling ending mixing revenga and joy.

Grose Pointe Blank

Offbeat romantic comedy set in a small town, with great lead performance by John Cusack and many quotable moments.

Reservoir Dogs

The film that started the 90s indie film revolution? Crackling dialogue and almost cartoonish violence. Still Tarantino's best film, in my opinion.
